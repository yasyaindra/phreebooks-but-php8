Deleting a Customer Contact

NOTE: Customers cannot be deleted if they have a journal entry associated with them.

To delete a customer record:

  1. Select Customers->Maintain Customers page.
  2. Select the Delete icon next to the customer to delete. A search box has been provided to quickly locate the customer.
  3. Confirm the deletion and you will be returned to the Maintain Customers page.
