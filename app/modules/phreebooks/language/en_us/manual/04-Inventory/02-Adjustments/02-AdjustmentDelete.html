Inventory - Adjustments (Delete)

Delete inventory adjustment procedure

  1. From the main menu, select Inventory->Adjustments.
  2. Press the Open button to display the inventory adjustment popup window.
  3. Select the adjustment to delete by clicking anywhere on the row of the adjustment.
  4. Press the Delete button and confirm you selection to un-post the transaction from the journal.
