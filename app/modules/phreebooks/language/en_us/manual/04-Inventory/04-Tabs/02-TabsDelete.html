Inventory - Tabs (Category) (Delete)

WARNING: Deleting a tab will also delete all inventory fields that are part of that tab. To keep the fields, edit each inventory field and reassign them to a valid tab before deleting.

Deleting an Inventory tab (category)

  1. From the main menu select Inventory->Setup Inventory Field Tabs.
  2. Click anywhere on the row to highlight.
  3. Press the Edit button.
  4. Edit the inventory tab fields.
  5. Press the Delete button to delete and a confirmation will appear.
  6. Press the Delete button on the confirmation to delete the tab.
