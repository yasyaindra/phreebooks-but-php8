Inventory - Custom Fields (Delete)

WARNING: Deleting a inventory custom field will delete all inventory information stored in the targeted field. This action is not reversible.

Deleting an Inventory Custom Field

  1. From the main menu select Inventory->Setup Inventory Fields.
  2. Click anywhere on the row to highlight.
  3. Press the Edit button.
  4. Press the Delete button to delete and a confirmation will appear.
  5. Press the Delete button on the confirmation to delete the custom field.
