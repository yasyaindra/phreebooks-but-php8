Reports/Forms Security

Phreebooks reports and forms have three levels of security: user, employee, and department. They my be used individually or in any combination.

User security - One or more (or all by default) users may be given access to the report.

Employee - One of more (or all by default) employees may be given access to the report.

Department - one or more (or all by default) departments may be given access to the report.
