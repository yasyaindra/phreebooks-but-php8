Vendors - Contacts

Vendor contacts contain information about your suppliers of inventory, services and other purchases required for your business. The procedures to setup, edit and delete vendor contacts is similar to that for customers within PhreeBooks.

Vendor Account features:

  • Default global settings for purchase account and terms; customizable for each individual vendor
  • Unlimited billing addresses
  • Unlimited shipping addresses
  • Individaul purchase rep default assignment
  • Freee-form notes field
