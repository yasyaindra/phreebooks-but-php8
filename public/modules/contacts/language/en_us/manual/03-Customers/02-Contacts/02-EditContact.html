Editing a Customer Contact

To edit a customer record:

  1. Select Customers->Maintain Customers page.
  2. Select the Edit icon next to the customer to edit. A search box has been provided to quickly locate the customer.
  3. Edit/update/change any fields for the customer.
  4. Add/Delete any additional shipping or billing addresses.
  5. Press the Save button to save the changes. You will be returned to the Maintain Customers page.
