Inventory - Custom Fields (Edit)

Editing a Custom Inventory Field

  1. From the main menu select Inventory->Setup Inventory Fields.
  2. Click anywhere on the row to highlight.
  3. Press the Edit button.
  4. Edit the custom inventory fields.
  5. Press Update to save your changes.
