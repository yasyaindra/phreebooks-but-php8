Banking - Cash Register

PhreeBooks provides a view of the current status of your cash accounts. The register shows all deposits and withdrawals for a given accounting period, including future posted transactions.

To View Your Cash Account

  1. Select Banking -> Bank Account Register.
  2. The screen will display a beginning balance for the period and all transactions that were posted for the period.
  3. To change cash accounts, select a new account from the GL Account pull down menu.
