Shipping Modules

PhreeBooks has a built in Shipping Manager module to aid in the generation of rates and labels for your products. Additional modules may be added to further enhance the number of shipping options. A shipping log is kept with pertinent inforamton to monitor your packages and verify status.

Standard Shipping Modules

Federal Express (FedEx) - Includes rates, label generation, tracking, and logging

United Parcel Service (UPS) - Includes rates, label generation, tracking, and logging

United States Postal Service (USPS) - rates, logging

Flat Rate Shipping - rates, logging

Free Shipping - rates, logging

Per Item Shipping - rates, logging

Store Pickup (Will Call) - rates, logging

Table Rate Based Shipping - rates, logging

Zone (State/Province) Based Shipping - rates, logging
